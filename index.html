The way we work

You do the choosing. We do the chasing.

You should not have to carry fabric bolts up an elevator, remeasure your own window at 11pm, or hunt the tailor down for a Tuesday delivery. Four steps, in the same order, every time — each one designed to take a decision off your plate.

  1. 01 · Visit or Call

    Start where you are.

    See the swatch wall in person, or send a photo on WhatsApp. Both routes end in the same shortlist.

  2. 02 · Free Measurement

    We come to you.

    Free measurement across Hyderabad. We bring the ladder, the ideas, and enough restraint to say when you don't need something.

  3. 03 · Fabric & Finish

    Choose without overwhelm.

    Four thousand fabrics narrowed to five. You pick from five, not from four thousand.

  4. 04 · Stitch & Install

    Delivered and mounted.

    Five to seven days. Our two-man team hangs it, squares it, and leaves nothing loose. If a drop is a finger off, we come back the same week.

Why it hangs straight

Nothing is farmed out.

The reason a curtain sags six months in — or a sofa cover stops meeting its zip — is that three vendors handled it and none of them own the fault. We do the measure, the cut, the stitch, the mount, the alteration. When you call, you don't get transferred.
