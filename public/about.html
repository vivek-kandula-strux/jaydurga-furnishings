Our story
What began in 1984 as a single-room curtain shop on Ameerpet's main street has stayed on the same street ever since. Same family, same doorway, quietly widened over four decades into eight categories under one roof.
How we pick fabric
Every roll on our floor was chosen by hand by someone in the family — from Panipat, Karur, Bhilwara and Erode. We buy what falls right, what wears well and what looks honest against a Hyderabad wall.
Why one showroom, not many
You shouldn't drive across the city for a curtain, then again for a carpet, then again for a mattress. Everything a home needs from the floor up is here, on one floor. Bring a swatch, bring a photo — we'll walk you through it.
Our promise to you
No hard sell, no online store, no shipping surprises. Walk in, see it, touch it, take it home. If something needs stitching, cutting or fitting, we handle that too — the way we have since 1984.
