Getting here

Three quiet ways to arrive.

  1. i.

    By Metro

    Alight at Ameerpet Metro Station. From there it is a short walk along the main road to Mandhana Towers. The showroom is on the fourth floor.

  2. ii.

    By car

    Visitor parking is available in the Mandhana Towers basement. Take the lift to the fourth floor — we are right as you step out.

  3. iii.

    By auto

    Tell the driver "Mandhana Towers, Ameerpet." Head to the fourth floor — Jaydurga Furnishings is signposted at the lift.
