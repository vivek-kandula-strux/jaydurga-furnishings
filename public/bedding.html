What we do with bedding

Every set on our shelves has been through the wash.

For forty-two years we have washed every bedding line before it reaches our shelves. We look at the shrinkage after ten cycles, the pilling on the sateen, the seam strength on the fitted corners. If the sheet does not still feel right at wash twenty, it doesn’t stay.

We are the authorised distributor for several of India’s leading bedding mills. That means the thread count on the label is the thread count in the weave — verified, not marketed.
